Lets Introduce

For over 40 years, Cook Italian has been one of St.james best Italian restaurants and the backdrop of family gatherings, first dates and large celebrations. Located in the historic Italian neighborhood known as "The Hill," Cook Italian's exudes a timeless charm and captures the essence of Italian hospitality. Our talented chefs transcend trends with a menu of handmade pastas bursting with vibrant flavors, authentic fresh seafood creations, high-quality meat dishes, and, of course, our signature toasted raviolis. A fine dining experience without pretense, we invite you to enjoy the warm and inviting ambience of Cook Italian's on the Hill. No matter what the occasion, our staff will ensure that your time with us is truly unforgettable.
